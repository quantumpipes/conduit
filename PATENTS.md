## Covered Innovations

The following innovations are covered by this patent grant:

- **Internal service mesh orchestration.** Automated discovery, routing, and
  health-aware load balancing of services within air-gapped infrastructure,
  without requiring external service registries or cloud APIs.

- **Integrated DNS-TLS certificate automation.** Coordinated provisioning of
  internal DNS records and TLS certificates from a local certificate authority,
  enabling zero-configuration HTTPS for internal services with no internet
  dependency.

- **GPU health monitoring and container inspection.** Unified hardware health
  telemetry (CPU, memory, disk, GPU utilization) combined with container-level
  inspection for AI workload scheduling and capacity planning in offline
  environments.
